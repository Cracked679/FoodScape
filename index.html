About Us

Welcome to FoodScape

FoodScape stands for happiness and light, contemporary designed and influenced by ancient Indian traditions, FoodScape offers a sophisticated dining and takeaway experience. With its intoxicating mix of old and new setting, its effortlessly charming staff and its tantalising food will make you want to come back for more.

Services

Good food has the ability to delight your sense of taste and leave you happier.Each menu item is specifically tailored with flair and attention to detail is characteristic of FoodScape for every occasion, whether it be a large dinner party, family outing, intimate first date. Regardless of the occasion, you can always expect a great atmosphere with great food at the FoodScape.
