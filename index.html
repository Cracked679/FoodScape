Welcome to FoodScape

FoodScape stands for happiness and light, contemporary designed and influenced by ancient Indian traditions, FoodScape offers a sophisticated dining and takeaway experience. With its intoxicating mix of old and new setting, its effortlessly charming staff and its tantalising food will make you want to come back for more.
